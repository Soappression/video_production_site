This is my newest gaming project, Soappression.  This youtube channel houses montages, retrospectives, and anything else gaming related that I have made.  I've always wanted to upload my own gameplay in some form, always trying to replicate what I was interested in watching.  It started out with a youtube channel with the handle, "killsteeler12804" my gamertag on Playstation, I uploaded minecraft lets plays, call of duty commentaries, you name it!  I went through many different channels throught my life, trying to find a niche that suited me.  After growing up however, I found that I dont need a niche to create content, I just need to enjoy the content I'm making, be that short films, gaming videos, or websites like this one.  So now I have Soappression, my latest attempt at posting gaming videos on the internet!

Recent Production

My most recent upload on Soappression was this Call of Duty montage showing off the AEK Marksman Rifle with the full auto modification.  I decided to put the little scene where I talk about the gun at the begining of the video instead of the end, there was no reason for this other than seeing if it flowed better.  The song I used as a backing track was modified just a little bit, I used Premiere's audio looping setting to run the track to length.  The song was really good until I did this, I felt like it got pretty repetative aftaer a minute or two.  I also tried a few new things with the light leak transitions and flashing between actual gameplay and play of the game replays.  All and all I did enjoy the project even if it was just a little COD montage!  I don't exactly intent on making more montages, but I have so much footage from BO6 from before I deleted it that I almost feel bad letting the footage go to waste, so who know!

What's next?

The production I currently have in the works is a little outside of my comfort zone, but I really loved the idea as soon as it came to me.  The concept is about how the world is supposed to be this colorful magical place all throughout our lives, but due to different experiences and desires, it becomes this gloomy black and white world of boredom.  I plan on calling the piece, "Normality" and through it you will see the main character, a young man, struggles with losing his passions and becoming another "zombie" in this world that everyone tells him is normal.  The idea came to me as I was at work and talking to myself about adhd, anxiety, and autism.
